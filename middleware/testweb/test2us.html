About the role

We're building the infrastructure layer for the next generation of AI systems. You'll work directly with our research team to take models from prototype to production, owning the full ML lifecycle from data pipelines through deployment and monitoring.

What you'll do

  • Lead model optimization: quantization, distillation, inference speed improvements

  • Collaborate with research to productionize novel architectures
  • Mentor 2–3 junior ML engineers on the platform team
Requirements
  • 5+ years ML engineering experience with production deployments
  • Strong Python, PyTorch/JAX; experience with distributed training (FSDP, DeepSpeed)
  • Experience with MLOps tooling: Kubeflow, MLflow, or equivalent
  • Bonus: publications or open-source contributions in ML infrastructure
